خبث ḳabuta u (ḳubt, خباثة) to be bad; to be wicked, evil, malicious, vicious, malignant <b>VI</b> to behave viciously, display malice; to feel awkward, feel embarrassed


خبث ḳubt badness, wickedness; malignancy (e.g., of a disease); malice, malevolence, viciousness


خبث ḳabat refuse, scum, dross, slag
