ساس (سوس) sāsa u (سياسة siyāsa) to dominate, govern, rule (ه s.o.): to lead, guide, conduct, direct (هـ, ه s.o., s.th.); to administer, manage, regulate (هـ s.th.); -- sawisa yaswasu to be or become worm-eaten; to become carious (tooth); to rot, decay (bones) <b>II</b> and <b>V</b> = sawisa


سوس sūs (coll.: n. un. ة) pl. سيسان sīsān woodworm, borer; mothworm
