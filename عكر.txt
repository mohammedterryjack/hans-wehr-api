عكر ‘akira a (‘akar) to be or become turbid, muddy, roily <b>II</b> to render turbid, to roil, to muddy (هـ or على s.th.); to disturb, trouble (هـ or على s.th.) │ عكر الصفو (ṣafwa) to destroy the untroubled state, disturb the order; عكر صفوه (ṣafwahū) and عكر عليه الصفو (ṣafwa) to kill s.o.’s good spirits, spoil s.o.’s good humor <b>V</b> to become turbid, become muddy; to deteriorate, be aggravated, become worse (situation)


عكر ‘amr turbidity, muddiness; sediment, dregs, lees
